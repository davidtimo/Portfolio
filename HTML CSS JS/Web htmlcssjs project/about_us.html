About CHocs

CHocs is a famous chocolate brand in Indonesia founded in 2018. It was first established in Jakarta and currently already has two other branches which are in Bandung and Sanur. CHocs specializes in many variants of chocolate products, especially dark chocolate which the owner really loves.

History

CHocs was first started as a small chocolate shop in Jakarta. Our mission is to make various kind of chocolate products for the customers to enjoy. After years of development, we managed to expand our business and established two branches in Bandung and Sanur with Jakarta as the current head office.
